# Blocked page artwork

The pixel-art Barad-dûr tower is **original artwork** generated by
[`scripts/generate-pixel-tower.ts`](../../scripts/generate-pixel-tower.ts).

Multi-layer 96×140 pixel composition: mountain ground, tiered fortress, horn crown,
static eye socket/slit, animated gate/lava fire (SVG SMIL), and a mouse-tracked iris layer.

Layout mood is inspired by classic pixel fantasy art (e.g.
[Pixel Baraddur by wedontneed](https://www.deviantart.com/wedontneed/art/Pixel-Baraddur-846756725),
[Top of Barad-Dur on Pixel Art Maker](https://pixelartmaker.com/art/d6d4b3a82c14158)).
No third-party pixel assets are embedded.
