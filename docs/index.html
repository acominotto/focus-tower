A Chrome extension inspired by Cold Turkey — with a watchful tower at the gate.

When your hand reaches for another tab, the tower stands in your path. Not to punish you — to pause the drift and ask whether this visit is worth the hour that follows.

The gate

Blocked sites redirect to a gate screen: the same volcanic sky, the same dark tower, and a quote that nudges you back toward what matters. You can take a timed break or turn away.

The eye

Grant yourself a short break and a small watcher appears on allowed sites — the tower’s eye tracking your session until the gate closes again. The metaphor is simple: permission is temporary; attention is precious.

  • Default block list for common distractions, fully customizable
  • Inspiring quotes — built-in library plus your own
  • Time-on-site stats with achievement comparisons
  • 5, 10, or 30 minute breaks, or allow for the session
  • English, French, German, Italian, and Spanish
  • Local only — no accounts, no cloud sync
